Use ninja_prep on a series of linear reference sequences with numerical headers (fasta format). 
See main readme and formats_readme.txt in the root of NINJA-OPS as well as the built-in ninja_prep usage information.

You can specify ranges, sorts, etc in the commandlines. 

Region trimming for V4 (EMP primers?) and V3 built-in for greengenes 13_8 97% only.
